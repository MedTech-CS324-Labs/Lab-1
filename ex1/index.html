France

France is known for its rich culture, art, and cuisine. From the Eiffel Tower to the French Riviera, it attracts millions of tourists each year.

Popular Attractions

  • Paris – Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum
  • Nice – French Riviera beaches
  • Versailles – Palace of Versailles

Japan

Japan blends modern technology with traditional culture. Visitors enjoy futuristic cities, serene temples, and stunning natural landscapes.

Popular Attractions

  • Tokyo – Shibuya Crossing, Tokyo Tower
  • Kyoto – Temples, cherry blossoms
  • Mount Fuji – Iconic mountain

Egypt

Egypt offers ancient wonders, from the pyramids to the Nile River. Its long history makes it a fascinating travel destination.

Popular Attractions

  • Cairo – Pyramids of Giza, Sphinx
  • Luxor – Valley of the Kings
  • Aswan – Nile River cruises
